Who We Are

Built by
Students

The Morristown High School Engineering Club is a student-run organization for anyone passionate about how things work and how to make them better. Whether you're into robotics, coding, design, or just curious about engineering, you belong here.

We work on real projects, learn from each other, and have a great time doing it. No prior experience required — just a willingness to build and explore.
